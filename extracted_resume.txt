B. Digitization and Scalability: Technical Excellence & Operational Efficiency

Process Improvement 1 (Data Automation & Cost Savings)

Challenge: Identified a labor-intensive, manual data entry process (requiring 1 FTE) that introduced workflow bottlenecks, high labor cost, and human error risk across all assessments.

Action & Result: Successfully led the full deployment of a 100% digitized workflow for assessments, including the SINGER extension and various psychometric scales. This automation eliminated the need for a dedicated data entry officer, delivering an immediate SGD50,000 annual cost saving (1 FTE). Crucially, this new digital infrastructure creates a highly scalable model that prevents the need for additional manpower investment, even as the volume of studies and service provision scales up.

Technical System Contribution (Qualtrics Library)

Scenario: The shift from error-prone, hardcopy administration and manual rater scoring to fully digitized data required a robust, standardized, and reusable technical asset library for neuropsychological assessments (NPAs) to support both research and NCC services.

Action & Result: Designed and implemented a comprehensive Qualtrics assessment template library for core NPAs (RBANS, NTB, VDB, ADAS-COG, etc.) used within MACC. This technical asset eliminated the error-prone hardcopy administration and manual scoring steps for these assessments, replacing them with automated, in-platform data capture and scoring logic. This critical shift eliminates all future manpower required for questionnaire creation and manual scoring, enabling a scalable deployment model for future studies and NCC services. Strategic Validation Example: The library has already secured its first external utilization, with Dr. Saima's team (part of the INTERREACH grant) interested in utilizing the VDB (Vascular Dementia Battery) template. While this initial, early-stage collaboration is not revenue-generating, it serves as crucial strategic validation of the asset's quality, establishes a collaborative service model, and lays the groundwork for future cost-recovery or revenue streams from non-affiliated external partners. This standardized technical asset maintains the 100% digitized workflow, delivering consistently cleaned data within the target 5 working days of assessment completion and significantly reducing rater error and subsequent query resolution time.
